Problem statement:
Modern day students have to live away from their homes to study in colleges. Limited finances are provided to the student that
create a need for effective money management application that can track expenses, manage budget and provide finance awareness.
Unplanned spending of money leads to overspending and unnecessary purchase of goods which are of no use.

Scope of the project:
This application provides a platform through which student finances can be handled. Students can track their expenses, create a budget by providing a monthly limit,and review their financial insights.
It can become a college buddy for students because it can make their life easier and eliminate the stress caused due to money management problems.
The application not only manages finances but also provide awareness within the student which are helpful to them in their future life.   
The total student population in India as a percentage of the total population is approximately 20% which create a large scope for this application.
Moreover, the scope of this application can be increased by focusing on the entire populataion stating it as an application that any person can manage their finances efficiently.   

Target Users:The main focus at present is on the students, but can be increased by including people of any age group in the population.

High-level features:

1.Increases Financial awareness among students .

2.User authentication feature available in the application that let user enter their username, email and password. 
When the user try to login, the application asks for the username and password, 
if the details entered are correct then the login successful message is displayed otherwise the access to the application is denied. 

3.Budget Management: Students can set limit to their expences by creating a monthly budget. An alert message is displayed if expences reach the limit.

Helps students save money for future use and emergencies.
Students can trust this application because it does not ask for account details or any personal information. Also, it provides a user authentication system that provides access only to the defined user.
